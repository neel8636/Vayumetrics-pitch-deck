EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Vayumetrics

Mission: Autonomous drone scanning + computer vision for continuous inventory accuracy and a living 3D digital twin of the warehouse.

Problem

Manual cycle counts are labor-intensive, disrupt operations, and still miss misplacements. Safety risks persist at height, audits lack spatial context, and multi-site visibility is stale.

Solution

Docked drones patrol aisles on schedule or on demand. Edge CV captures barcodes, labels, and shelf state; a cloud pipeline reconciles against WMS and builds a spatially anchored 3D twin for analytics, audits, and slotting.

Market

TAM $59.6B Warehouse automation by 2030
SAM $8.4B Warehouse management software (adjacent)
SOM ~$30M Beachhead ≈ 500 sites × ~$60k ACV

Business Model

  • Vision: $18–24k base + usage (cap); hardware as-a-service optional.
  • Pro (LiDAR): $36k+ base + usage; higher coverage/accuracy for tough sites.
  • Pilot: 90-day paid, opt-to-buy credit to Year-1; clear success criteria.

Competition & Edge

Incumbents prove the category. Vayumetrics is software-first—vision accuracy, 3D twin, exception workflows, and WMS sync; partner-agnostic hardware to minimize capex.
