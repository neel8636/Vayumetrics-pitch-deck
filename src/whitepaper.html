Vayumetrics – Executive Summary Confidential

Autonomous inventory, real-time visibility, spatial 3D twin

Vayumetrics automates cycle counts using a docked drone that scans pallet positions overnight. Our software-first pipeline reconciles against the WMS and maintains a living 3D twin of the warehouse, surfacing exceptions with photo proof.

Problem

  • Manual counts are slow, disruptive, and still miss misplacements.
  • Leads to quarter-end firefighting: expedites, overtime, and write-offs.
  • No current, spatial view across sites for managers to act on.

Solution

  • Night scans on schedule or on demand; zero daytime disruption.
  • Vision reads labels & shelf state; Pro adds LiDAR for difficult sites.
  • Reconciles to WMS; morning exception list with images and locations.
